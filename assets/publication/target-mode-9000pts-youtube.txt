* Description

This is the tas of one of the four mini games, target mode.
You play Roberto and you have to score by shooting on panels.
You have to finish the history with Roberto on the hardest difficulty to unlock it.

* Memory address

- (Word) 1C681C : Score
- (Word) 1F1F5E : Panel Points
- (Word) 1F1F60 : Art Points
- (Word) 1F1F62 : Frame Points
- (Word) 1EFA70 : (Direction) Up / Down
- (Word) 1EFA6E : (Direction) Left / Right

* Panels

500 | 300 | 200 | 300 | 500
400 | 300 | 200 | 300 | 400
300 | 200 | 100 | 200 | 300

* Points

- Panel Points : When panels (between 1 and 4 by shoot) are smashed
- Art Points : When the ball is curved
- Frame Points : When a frame is knocked
